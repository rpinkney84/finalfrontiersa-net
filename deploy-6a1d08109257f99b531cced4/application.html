What We Look For

Final Frontier holds its members to a high standard. Here's what it takes to earn a place in our ranks.

Consistent raid attendance, active participation, and a genuine passion for high-level play.

Respect for fellow members, willingness to help, and a collaborative spirit both in and out of raid.

Growth Mindset

Always looking to improve your performance, learn the meta, and adapt to new content.

Honesty, loyalty, and conduct that upholds the Final Frontier name — in game and in the DAO.
