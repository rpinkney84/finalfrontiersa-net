Contest Rules & Guidelines

General Rules

  • All participants must be members of the Final Frontier community or follow us on social media as specified per contest.
  • Participants must be 18 years of age or older, or have parental consent where required by local law.
  • One entry per person per contest unless otherwise specified. Multiple accounts to gain extra entries are prohibited.
  • Winners are selected fairly — by random draw for giveaways, or by verifiable performance metrics for skill competitions.
  • Prizes are distributed within 14 days of winner announcement. Unclaimed prizes forfeit after 30 days.
  • Final Frontier reserves the right to disqualify participants found cheating, using exploits, or violating community standards.
  • Decisions made by Final Frontier leadership regarding contest outcomes are final.
  • Participation constitutes agreement to these rules and to our Privacy Policy.
