Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle information across our platforms and services.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024  |  Effective Date: January 1, 2022  |  Organization: Final Frontier Gamers DAO LLC (Wyoming)

Overview

Final Frontier Gamers DAO LLC ("Final Frontier," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website finalfrontiersa.net and associated platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our community.

By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, Discord username, and other details you voluntarily provide via contact forms or guild applications.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, IP address, and referral source (collected automatically via standard web analytics).
  • Social Media Data: When you interact with our social channels (Twitter, Discord, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook), those platforms may collect data per their own privacy policies.
  • Blockchain / Wallet Data: Public wallet addresses you choose to share for guild participation or event rewards. We do not store private keys.
  • Communications: Messages sent to us via email or contact forms.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website and guild platforms
  • Process guild membership applications and communicate with applicants
  • Send updates about guild events, tournaments, and announcements
  • Analyze website usage to improve our user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our community standards
  • Distribute rewards, prizes, or contest winnings to eligible participants

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our website (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.
  • Community Safety: To prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect the safety of our members.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Embedded widgets (Twitter feed, TradingView charts, CoinMarketCap ticker) may set their own cookies per their privacy policies.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Report suspected security vulnerabilities to support@finalfrontiersa.net.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites including Star Atlas, Solana ecosystem tools, crypto exchanges, and social media platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@finalfrontiersa.net.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
