Midnight

The Quel'Thalas Saga Begins

Final Frontier's complete field guide to WoW: Midnight — covering every dungeon, raid wing, weapon tier, class build, and lore thread your guild needs to conquer the Void's advance on the Blood Elves' ancient homeland.

Expansion Overview

Welcome to Quel'Thalas

WoW: Midnight returns players to the sunswept spires of Silvermoon and the surrounding wilds of Quel'Thalas — now corrupted by Void energies as the ancient dark tears through the Sunwell's light once more.

New Zones

Silvermoon Reborn, the Ghostlands Awakened, Isle of Quel'Danas, and the Voidshattered Sunwell Plateau — 6 open-world zones with dynamic day/night mechanics.

New Systems

The Arcane Resonance system replaces borrowed power with permanent character progression. Sunwell Shards power new crafting recipes unavailable anywhere else.

Allied Races

Void Elves and Blood Elves receive major story arcs and new heritage armor sets. A new allied race — the Sunfury Elves — joins the Alliance after Midnight's campaign.

Level Cap

Level cap raised to 90. New talent rows at 85 and 90 add a final capstone ability to every specialization, dramatically changing high-end gameplay.

"They think they can extinguish our light — but we are the children of the Sunwell, and we do not break. We burn." — Lor'themar Theron, Regent Lord of Quel'Thalas
Instance Guide

Dungeons of Quel'Thalas

Eight new dungeons span the breadth of Midnight — from the corrupted streets of Silvermoon to the Void-choked ruins beneath the Sunwell. Full Wowhead links included for each encounter.

Silvermoon: The Shattered Spire
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

The jewel of Quel'Thalas has fallen to Void corruption. Fight through the Grand Magister's sanctum to the spire summit and cleanse the Void crystal before it tears open a portal.

Wowhead Guide
Ghostlands: Deatholme Reborn
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

The undead fortress of Deatholme now serves a Void master. Plunge into its necrotic depths to sever the ley line powering a Void beacon threatening to blind the Sunwell forever.

Wowhead Guide
Sunwell: Outer Reaches
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

The outer rings of the Sunwell Isle are overrun. Clear the Void-touched naaru fragments from the three sanctum wings to restore the protective aura shielding the inner sanctum.

Wowhead Guide
Eversong: The Tainted Grove
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

Once a vibrant forest, the Eversong Woods now bleeds shadow. A Void Dryad has seduced the ancient treants into corruption — only the Sunfire can burn it away.

Wowhead Guide
Arcane Sanctum of Quel'evar
Heroic Only • 5-Player

A hidden mage tower now serves as the Void's academy, corrupting Blood Elf arcanists into Void Weavers. Shut down the dark ley engine before its ritual completes.

Wowhead Guide
The Void Between Spires
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

A dungeon that exists partly in the Void itself — some corridors phase between realities. Navigate shifting geometry and defeat a Herald of the Old Gods before he ascends.

Wowhead Guide
The Sunfire Vaults
Mythic+ Available • 5-Player

Beneath Silvermoon's royal district lie the Sunfire Vaults — treasury and armory of the ancient Highborne. Void Thieves raid its artifacts to power their dark engines. Stop them.

Wowhead Guide
Naxxanar: The Void Cradle
Mythic+ Available • Season 2

A Nerubian-Void hybrid fortress has risen in the far north of Quel'Thalas. Its master — a Void-touched Nerubian queen — weaves a web that threatens to envelop all of northern Azeroth.

Wowhead Guide
Raid Content

Raids of the Midnight Tier

Two massive raid instances define Midnight's endgame, culminating in a confrontation at the heart of the Sunwell itself.

Tier 1 Raid • 8–12 Bosses

Citadel of the Fallen Sun

The first major raid of Midnight. The former Sunwell Citadel has been rebuilt by Void engineers into a dark fortress of twisted arcane-Void technology. Eight bosses stand between your raid and the inner sanctum.

  • Grand Arcanist Vel'naas
  • The Twin Void Seers
  • Arcanix the Unbound
  • Shalasyr the Shattered
  • Void Nexus Construct
  • High Inquisitor Dar'khan
  • The Void Council (3)
  • N'zalis the Unseen
